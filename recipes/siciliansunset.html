Sicilian Sunset



This colorful Sicilian-style drink will brighten up any party. You can also make it non-alcoholic by replacing prosecco with Sprite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup Prosecco or Sprite
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup cranberry juice
  • 2 lemons, zested

Steps

  1. Place ice in a glass pitcher.
  2. Pour in Prosecco or Sprite, orange juice, and cranberry juice.
  3. Stir
  4. Pour into champagne flutes, and sprinkle with lemon zest to serve.
  5. Enjoy!
