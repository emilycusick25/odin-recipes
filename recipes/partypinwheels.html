Party Pinwheels



Home

Party pinwheels are a crowd favorite for any party or family gathering. They are easy and simple to make and packed full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 8oz packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 oz package ranch dressing mix
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 4 12 inch flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1 2oz can sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Mix together cream cheese, ranch mix, and green onions in a medium bowl until combined
  2. Spread onto each tortilla. Sprinkle evenly with cheese, red pepper, celery, and olives.
  3. Roll up tortillas then wrap each tightly in aluminum foil.
  4. Chill in refridgerator, 2 hours to overnight.
  5. To serve, unwrap each rolled tortilla; trim ends and slice into 1 inch pieces.
  6. Enjoy!
